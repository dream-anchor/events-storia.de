Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What catering options does STORIA offer?

STORIA offers Italian finger food, antipasti platters, warm buffets with pasta and casseroles, Neapolitan pizzas, and desserts in jars. All dishes are freshly prepared in our kitchen in Munich Maxvorstadt.

Can events be held at STORIA restaurant?

Yes, STORIA restaurant can accommodate up to 100 guests indoors and another 100 on the covered terrace. We host corporate events, Christmas parties, team events, business dinners, and private celebrations.

How short-notice can I order catering?

For catering orders, we recommend at least 24 hours advance notice. Pizza orders can often be accommodated on shorter notice. For urgent requests, please contact us by phone.

Are there vegetarian or vegan options?

Yes, we offer numerous vegetarian dishes such as Caprese, Parmigiana di Melanzane, Pasta Primavera, and vegetarian pizzas. Vegan options are available on request.

What is STORIA Catering's delivery area?

We deliver throughout Munich and surrounding areas. Within 1 km (Maxvorstadt), delivery is free for orders over €50. For longer distances, we charge a distance-based delivery fee.

Is the food delivered warm?

Warm dishes such as casseroles and pasta are delivered in warming containers (chafing dishes). Pizzas are delivered fresh from the oven. Cold items like finger food and antipasti are packed chilled.

Can custom menus be created?

Yes, we are happy to create individual menus for your event. Contact us with your wishes regarding number of guests, budget, and preferences, and we will provide a tailored offer.

Does STORIA offer dessert catering?

Yes, we offer homemade desserts in jars such as tiramisu, panna cotta, mascarpone cream, and seasonal creations. The portion jars are ideal for events and buffets.
